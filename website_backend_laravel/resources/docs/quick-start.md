# Quick Start Guide

Learn the basics of using VerseVision to manage your worship service.

![Dashboard Overview](/docs/screenshots/quick-start-dashboard.png)


## The Dashboard

When you launch the app, you will see the operator interface. This is your command center.

- **Recent Songs**: Quickly access songs you've edited or played recently.
- **Quick Actions**: Buttons to add a new song, start a schedule, or go live.
- **System Status**: Check your cloud connection and AI credit balance.

## Adding Your First Song

1.  Navigate to the **Songs** tab in the sidebar.
2.  Click the **"Add Song"** button.
3.  Enter the Title and Author.
4.  Type or paste the lyrics into the editor. VerseVision uses a simple format:
    ```
    [Verse 1]
    Amazing grace how sweet the sound
    That saved a wretch like me
    ```
5.  Click **Save**.

## Creating a Schedule

A schedule is a list of songs and media for a specific service.
1.  Go to the **Schedule** tab.
2.  Click **"New Schedule"**.
3.  Drag and drop songs from your library into the schedule area.
4.  Reorder them as needed.

## Going Live

To start projecting:
1.  Open your schedule or a specific song.
2.  Click the **"Go Live"** button.
3.  The output window will appear on your secondary display.
4.  Click on a verse to display it.
