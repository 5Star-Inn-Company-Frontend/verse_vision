# Introduction

Welcome to the VerseVision documentation. VerseVision is a powerful software solution designed to enhance your worship experience through seamless lyric projection, AI-powered features, and robust media management.

## What is VerseVision?

VerseVision allows churches and worship teams to:
- **Scripture Detection**: Our intelligent engine listens to the preacher/speaker and automatically displays the referenced scripture in real-time.
- **Real-time Translation**: Translate your service in real-time for a multilingual congregation.
- **Multi-Camera Grid**: Connect multiple smartphone cameras via WebRTC for a professional broadcast experience without expensive hardware.
- **Project Lyrics**: Display songs and hymns with beautiful backgrounds.
- **AI Integration**: Automatically generate background images and search for song lyrics using AI.
- **Media Management**: Organize your song library, backgrounds, and schedules.
- **Live Streaming**: Integrate with OBS and other streaming tools.
