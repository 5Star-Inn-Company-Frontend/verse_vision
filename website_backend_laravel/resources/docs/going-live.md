Projecting content to your screens.

## The Live Button
Click the **Go Live** button in the top right to enable external output.

## Navigation
- **Arrow Keys**: Move between slides.
- **Number Keys**: Jump to specific sections (Verse 1, Chorus, etc.).
- **Clear Button**: Temporarily hide text (leaving the background).
- **Black Button**: Fade to black.
