# Troubleshooting

Common issues and solutions.

## Connection Issues
- **Cloud Sync Failed**: Check your internet connection and firewall settings.
- **Mobile App Not Pairing**: Ensure both devices are on the exact same Wi-Fi network (no guest networks).

## Display Issues
- **Projector Not Showing**: Check Windows Display Settings. Ensure "Extend these displays" is selected, not "Duplicate".

## AI Errors
- **Limit Reached**: Check your plan limits in the Dashboard.
