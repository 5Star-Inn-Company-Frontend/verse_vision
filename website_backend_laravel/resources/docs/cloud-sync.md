# Cloud Sync

Keep your data safe and accessible everywhere.

## How it Works
VerseVision automatically syncs your songs, schedules, and settings to the cloud when you are online.

## Benefits
- **Backup**: Never lose your library.
- **Collaboration**: Prepare schedules from home and sync them to the church computer.
- **Multi-Site**: Share resources across different campuses.
