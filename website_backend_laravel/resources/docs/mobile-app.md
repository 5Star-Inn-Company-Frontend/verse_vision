# Mobile Camera App

Turn your smartphone into a wireless camera.

![Mobile App Pairing Screen](/docs/screenshots/mobile-pairing.png)
> *Please upload a screenshot of the mobile app pairing QR code screen to: `public/docs/screenshots/mobile-pairing.png`*

## Setup
1. Download the **VerseVision Camera** app on iOS or Android.
2. Ensure your phone and computer are on the same Wi-Fi network.
3. Open the app and scan the QR code displayed in VerseVision **Settings > Mobile**.

## Usage
Use your phone as a roaming camera for baptisms, audience shots, or stage close-ups.
